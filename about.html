About Us

Enriching the young minds by giving exposure to the coding languages and future technologies.

Who we are?

SkillEdge Plus Academy is a government-recognized training institute, registered under the Ministry of Micro, Small & Medium Enterprises (MSME), Govt. of India.

Established in 2025, a progressive learning platform dedicated to empowering school and college students with essential technical skills. We focus on building strong foundations in programming, technology, and real-world problem-solving through hands-on workshops & practical trainings.

Our Story

SkillEdge Plus Academy was born from two simple observations:

  • Firstly, the young students who just decides to become engineers without any aim or guide. They are not fully aware of the coding languages that they have to learn before entering into the coding world. They take admissions but later find it difficult to learn these languages because they have not got the exposure to these languages before.
  • Secondly, the ones who are into the non-technical UG courses, but they want to learn some coding languages, so that they can have technical options too for their future career.

Founded by Dev Agnihotri, a tech enthusiast who loves developing simulations and also developed Virtual Labs Simulation in collaboration with prestigious institutes (IIT Kanpur). Our academy focuses on empowering young minds by exposing them to various coding languages and technologies through hands-on workshops and trainings that can help them enter into the field of Computer Science & Engineering with full confidence.

We believe that every student deserves access to quality tech education that is both affordable and effective. Our mission is to train the young minds with the emerging technologies and allow them to build a strong portfolio before entering the college.
